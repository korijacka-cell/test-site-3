Our Story

A family recipe.
A San Antonio favorite.

King of Shawarma and Kabab is a family-owned food truck and kitchen serving the rich, layered flavors of the Mediterranean to the heart of San Antonio. Every dish is homemade — from the slow-roasted shawarma cones to the muhammara, hummus, and Syrian garlic sauce.

What started as a dream of bringing real Mediterranean food to Texas has grown into a community staple. Halal, fresh, and served with the warmth of our family table.
